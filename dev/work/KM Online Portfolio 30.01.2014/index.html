*who am I ?

Hi! My name is Kamil and I like to spend time taping on the keyboard.
I love front-end and all the smart things one can do in Javascript. I appreciate functional / often minimalistic user interface and responsive design with uniform typography.

So here I am ... learning new things, programming and looking for opportunity to work with like minded individuals.

I also love vintage synthesizers, astrophotography, techie stuff, Eddie Izzard and Boards of Canada.
